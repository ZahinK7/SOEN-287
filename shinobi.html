Shinobis: Shadow Warriors


Shinobis or most famously called, ninjas, are secret warriors sent by warlords in feudal Japan.
They are one of the most feared warriors in the Land of the Rising Sun. They are specialized in espionage, messaging and surprise attacks.
They were sometime used also as bodyguards at night for warlords and generals to protect them from other Shinobis at night.

Shinobis uses a lot of equipments in order to complete their mission Most of the tools used by ninjas are modified farming tools.
Here are the tools they used:

  1. Weapons
    • Chain and sickle
    • Katana
    • Shuriken
  2. Clothes
    • Chain armor
    • Travel cape
    • Shozoku (black clothes)
  3. Equipment
    • Iron hooks
    • Smoke bombs
    • Iron caltrops
